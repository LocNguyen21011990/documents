Explain promotion case:
	- Without Qualifying Products : Discounted Products
	- With Amount of Qualifying Products : Khuyến mãi trên total của products (quantity*standard price)
	- With Number of Qualifying Products : Khuyến mãi trên quantity products
	- With Combination of Products : Mua n X and n Y --> khuyến mãi Z (X or Y)
	- Buy X/Get Y : Buy x or more Get n discounted product(s) (mua 3 sp --> khuyến mãi 1 trong 3)
	- Buy X and Y/Get Z : Mua X vs Y --> Khuyễn mãi Z
	- Buy X for Total : Mua 5 sp --> tính phí 3 sp (giá khuyến mãi),2sp con lai tính giá standard
